# PalmInsight — Product Overview

**An AI-powered palm reading web app that turns a phone photo into an instant, shareable, personalized reading — with a premium upgrade.**

Live: https://palm-drab.vercel.app · Status: Deployed, tested, payment-ready (test mode).

---

## 1. What it is

The user opens the site, takes a photo of their open palm, and within seconds gets a personalized reading of their palm lines. A genuine AI vision model **looks at the actual photo** — it first confirms the image is a real palm, then reads the individual lines and writes an interpretation unique to that hand. No two palms give the same result.

A free reading hooks the user; a one-time **$5 "Deep Report"** unlocks the high-intent content (money, marriage, children, destiny) and a daily lifestyle guide.

---

## 2. New features & highlights

**Real AI reading (not a gimmick).** Every photo is sent to a vision AI that validates it's a true palm and reads the lines from the image. If it isn't a palm (a selfie, an object, a pet), the AI says so instead of faking a result. Powered by OpenAI, with Anthropic and Google as automatic fallbacks so a single outage never breaks the product.

**Premium Deep Report — 6 personalized sections:**
- 🌌 Destiny · 💼 Career & Success · 💰 **Money & Wealth** · 💗 Love · 💍 **Marriage & Partnership** · 👶 **Family & Children**
- ✦ A catchy **"palm personality type"** plus **lucky number, color & day**

**You + Your Crush — compatibility.** Scan a partner's palm (or enter their birth date) for a match score with strengths, challenges, and advice. Built to be screenshotted and shared.

**Daily palm guidance.** A fresh, AI-written daily card personalized to each user's palm: a horoscope plus **what to wear (lucky color/outfit), what today is good for, what to avoid, your lucky time, and your mood.** Free as an in-app taste; delivered as a **daily email to paying members** (recurring engagement).

**Viral share card.** A polished Instagram-story image featuring the user's palm personality type and lucky highlights, branded with a "scan yours free" link — a built-in growth loop.

**Polish that sells.** A live "palm points analyzed" scanning animation, confidence meters per line, blurred premium previews (so users see exactly what they're missing), and celebratory haptics on unlock.

**Production-grade foundation.** Real payments (Polar), a real database (Supabase) so nothing is lost, user accounts + reading history, an admin analytics dashboard, rate limiting, and a clear privacy notice.

## 3. Target users

**Primary — Gen Z (18–30):** social-media natives and astrology/personality-test enthusiasts. This audience already shares zodiac signs, "which character are you" quizzes, and aesthetic results — palm reading fits perfectly and is highly visual.

**Secondary:**
- Casual curiosity users who try it once and share it
- Friends and couples comparing readings ("our match is 87%!")
- Content creators looking for shareable, on-trend content

Palm reading / "what your hands say about you" is a recurring viral trend on TikTok and Reels, which gives us a warm, ready audience.

---

## 4. How we pull (acquire) customers

**Built-in virality (lowest-cost growth):**
- Every reading produces a **gorgeous share card** with a "scan yours free" link — each user becomes a channel.
- **"You + Your Crush"** compatibility is inherently social — people tag and share match scores.
- Anonymous-first: a user gets a full free reading **with no signup**, removing all friction to that first wow moment.

**Conversion engine:**
- The free reading + a daily horoscope create the hook.
- **Blurred premium teasers** show the exact money/marriage/children content behind the paywall — high-intent FOMO drives the $5 unlock.
- One-time $5 is an easy impulse price.

**Retention & recurring value:**
- The **daily guidance email** brings paying users back every day with personalized "what to wear / what's good for you today" content — turning a one-time buyer into a daily habit.

**Channels to seed growth:**
- TikTok / Instagram Reels (palm-reading trend), influencer/creator seeding, astrology & self-discovery communities, Pinterest, and the organic share loop above.

**Economics:** AI cost per reading is a fraction of a cent, so the $5 unlock carries a very high margin, and free readings are cheap enough to fuel the viral loop profitably.
